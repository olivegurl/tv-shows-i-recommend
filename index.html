Historical Fiction

  1. The Crown: this is a great tv show for people who are interested in the royal family. There are 6 seasons, and although the sixth season was the most weak in my opinion, I still think it is a good show for lover of Downtown Abbey . It follows Queen Elizabeth II as she ascends to the throne after her father's death.

  2. My Lady Jane: a tv show that was recently cancelled, it follows a woman named Jane who is being forced to marry a man she doesn't know, Lord Dudley. Sounds like every other historical fiction tv show, right? Well, she also has to marry him to save her family from destitution, she finds out he's hot, and oh, there are magic beings called Ethians? A laugh-out-loud romp that was gone too soon. #SaveMyLadyJane
